## 💡 Key Contributions
* **Dynamic Gradient Masking (DGM):** Isolates specific parameter clusters responsible for localized knowledge properties, protecting baseline tasks during model modification.
* **Conflict-Aware Merging (CAM):** Resolves gradient direction contradictions when combining parallel edits across common network segments.
